# LaTeX Statement of Purpose (SoP) Template for Graduate Applications

This repository provides a **comprehensive LaTeX template** for crafting strong Statements of Purpose (SoP) for graduate school applications. The template is designed to guide students in presenting research experience, academic achievements, teaching/service, industry experience, and future research goals in a professional, polished format.

## Features

- **Structured Sections:** Predefined sections for research experience, academic excellence, teaching/service, industry experience, and future research interests.  
- **Inline Guidance:** Inline `% comments` provide clear instructions for writing and formatting each section.  
- **References & Publications:** Example formatting for citations, footnotes, and publications (journal/conference style).  
- **Consistency:** Uniform formatting to maintain readability and professional appearance.  
- **Customizable:** Easily replace placeholders with personal content, names, universities, and project details.  
- **Length Guidance:** Optimized for ~1â€“2 pages, keeping content concise while emphasizing achievements.  
- **Examples Included:** Sample research projects, publications, and SoP paragraphs that demonstrate strong writing style.
